Vision and Mission

Vision

Along with the vision of ITS to become an institution with an international reputation in science, technology, and art, the Department of Informatics aims to become a leading informatics innovator at the national and international levels. We emphasize supporting environmentally-friendly industries and maritime affairs and play an active role in advancing and prosper the nation

The vision of the Department of Informatics focuses on becoming an excellent and innovative-inventive educational institution in intelligent informatics, supporting digital transformation and contributing to society with an international reputation.

Mission

The missions of the Department of Informatics are:

1. Organizing a quality learning process, and meeting national and international standards,

2. Conducting innovative, quality, and useful research,

3. Increasing the use of information and communication technology for the community

4. Establishing partnerships with various institutions, both domestically and abroad.
